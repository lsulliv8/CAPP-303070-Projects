Lead Actor Bio

Born in 1980 and raised in New York City, Macaulay Culkin gained most of his fame from his roles as Kevin McCallister in Home Alone 1 and Home Alone 2. He is also known from Uncle Buck, My Girl, and the Pagemaster.
